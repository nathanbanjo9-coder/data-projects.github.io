Business Goal

Provide a clear executive view of performance: revenue, profit, MoM growth trends, and which drugs/customers are driving results (or dragging them down).

Key Questions

  • What is overall performance (revenue, profit, margin, COGS)?
  • Which products are most/least profitable?
  • Which customers are top and bottom performers?
  • How is MoM growth trending over time?
  • How does revenue break down by demographics and geography?

Key Findings

  • Strong profitability profile (high profit margin) with visible COGS tracking.
  • Clear top/bottom product visibility supports pricing, procurement, and product strategy decisions.
  • Customer ranking identifies where profit concentration and churn risk may exist.
  • MoM trend visual helps isolate spikes/dips for investigation (seasonality, promos, supply issues).

Recommendations

  • Protect supply and availability of top profit-driving drugs; review demand planning.
  • Investigate bottom products: margin erosion, high COGS, discounting, or low volumes.
  • Segment customer strategy: retain high-value customers; design uplift plans for mid-tier accounts.
  • Use MoM trend anomalies as triggers for root-cause drills (country, drug, customer segment).
