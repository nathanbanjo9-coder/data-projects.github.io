Executive Insights

Where attrition is highest

  • Sales department shows the highest attrition count.
  • Certain job roles (e.g., Sales Executive, Laboratory Technician) appear high-risk.

What correlates with attrition

  • Low income band employees leave more frequently.
  • Lower job satisfaction strongly correlates with attrition.

Recommendations

  • Improve retention incentives in Sales roles (targeted benefits, realistic quotas, manager coaching).
  • Review salary structure for low-income employees to reduce "flight risk" pressure.
  • Run engagement surveys and act on top satisfaction drivers (workload, leadership, growth).
  • Increase promotion / internal mobility paths for long-tenure staff.
