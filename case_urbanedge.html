Business Goal

Improve marketing efficiency by identifying which platforms and influencer categories deliver the best engagement and scale relative to spend.

Key Questions

  • Which platform drives the highest average views and follower scale?
  • What categories show the strongest engagement rate?
  • Which influencers are over-priced vs. performance?
  • How does performance vary by geography (country)?

Tooling

  • SQL: extraction + KPI shaping
  • Tableau: interactive dashboard
  • Metrics: engagement rate, views, followers, price/post, ROI
Open Tableau Dashboard View SQL Scripts

Highlights & Findings

Platform Performance (Views)

  • TikTok is the largest view driver (1,070,000) vs. Instagram (270,000), Twitch (250,000), YouTube (195,000).
  • Use platform-level performance to guide budget split.

Pricing vs. Value

  • Avg. price per post is £520 — benchmark influencers against engagement & follower scale.
  • Flag high-cost influencers with weak engagement for renegotiation or replacement.

Category Insight

  • Compare engagement rate by category (e.g., Fashion / Fitness / Food / Wellness) to find high-yield verticals.
  • Prioritise categories with strong engagement over pure follower count.

Geo Insight

  • Use the map view to identify markets with stronger engagement potential.
  • Localise influencer selection per country to reduce wasted spend.

Recommendations

  • Shift budget toward the highest view-driving platform(s) while maintaining controlled A/B tests on others.
  • Introduce a "value score" = engagement rate × followers ÷ price per post for fair comparisons.
  • Standardise pricing bands by platform + category to reduce overspend.
  • Build a shortlist of top performers per category for repeat campaigns.
